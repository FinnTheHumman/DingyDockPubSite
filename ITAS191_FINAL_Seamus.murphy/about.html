About Us

The Dinghy Dock Pub & Restaurant has been an attraction in Nanaimo, British Columbia since 1989. The breathtaking views, the laid back atmosphere and great pub feel make us a very popular destination year-round. We are the only registered floating pub in Canada, making everyone's experience a special one. We’re moored off beautiful Protection Island, originally known as Douglas Island. The ramp that connects the pub to the island allows visitors to walk the rock beaches, visit the parks or check out the community. Presently our docks range in size and fit vessels as small as kayaks or dingy and as large as a 50′ sail or powered boat. We have separate accommodations for minors, making the Dingy Dock accessible for the whole family. The Dinghy Dock Pub and Restaurant is completely floating, creating an amazing experience once aboard. Our menus offer classic pub grub and local brews from Vancouver Island.
